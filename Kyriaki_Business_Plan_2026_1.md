# Kyriaki (2026)
> *"Moving Oncology from Discovery to Enrollment."*

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Kyriaki is an oncology-focused AI agent platform that automates the end-to-end clinical trial enrollment process. Utilizing the OpenClaw local gateway for privacy-first data ingestion and Claude 4.6 Agent Teams for deep protocol reasoning, Kyriaki reduces the "Screen-to-Enroll" timeline from months to days. Our mission is to ensure no oncology patient dies waiting for paperwork to clear.

---

## 2. The Problem: The "Paperwork Marathon"

Clinical trial enrollment is currently a manual, document-heavy process.

- **The Data Gap:** Doctors lack the time to manually check every patient against 200+ exclusion criteria.
- **The Friction:** Patients find trials but get "lost in the sauce" of faxed records, HIPAA authorizations, and unreturned calls.
- **The Cost:** Pharma companies spend $50,000+ per patient in recruitment costs, yet 80% of trials fail to meet enrollment timelines.

---

## 3. The Solution: The Kyriaki Agent

Kyriaki is not a database; it is an **Acting Agent**. It doesn't just show a trial; it prepares the patient for entry.

### The Workflow

1. **Authorize:** Patient signs a digital consent via the Kyriaki OpenClaw Skill.
2. **Ingest:** Kyriaki pulls longitudinal records via TEFCA/QHIN (500M+ records reachable in 2026).
3. **Analyze:** A Claude 4.6 Agent Team performs parallel processing:
   - **Agent A:** Parses the 150-page Trial Protocol for "Hidden" exclusion logic.
   - **Agent B:** Cross-references the patient's genomics and lab history.
   - **Agent C:** Verifies "Lines of Therapy" requirements.
4. **Execute:** The agent auto-generates a Verified Eligibility Dossier and drafts the enrollment application for the trial site.

## 6. Regulatory & Compliance (The Moat)

Kyriaki is built to survive the 2026 FDA/HHS landscape:

- **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL):** The agent never clicks "Submit" without a biometric (FaceID) confirmation from the patient. This satisfies 21 CFR Part 11 attribution requirements.
- **Individual Access Service (IAS):** Under TEFCA, Kyriaki acts as the patient's legal representative, bypassing the need for slow B2B hospital integrations.
- **Digital Power of Attorney (DPOA):** A standardized legal layer that grants the Kyriaki Agent the authority to negotiate with trial coordinators.
